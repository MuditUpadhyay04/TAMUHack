Instructions for the Mock Interview:

  • Keep Your Video On: Ensure that your video remains turned on throughout the mock interview. This helps in simulating a real interview experience and allows for better feedback on non-verbal communication.
  • Be in a Quiet Environment: Choose a quiet and well-lit space where you can focus without interruptions. This will help you maintain a professional atmosphere.
  • Dress Professionally: Dress as you would for a real interview. This will help you get into the right mindset and make a good impression during the mock session.
  • Test Your Equipment: Before the session begins, check your camera, microphone, and internet connection to ensure everything works smoothly.
  • Be Punctual: Log in at least 5-10 minutes before the scheduled time. This demonstrates professionalism and ensures the session starts on time.
  • Prepare Like It's Real: Treat the mock interview as if it were an actual interview. Research the company/role (if applicable) and be ready to answer commonly asked questions.
  • Programming Knowledge Assessment: During the session, we will test your programming knowledge and skills. Be prepared to solve coding problems, explain your thought process, and demonstrate your technical expertise.
  • Active Participation: Engage with the interviewer actively. Listen carefully, ask clarifying questions when needed, and provide thoughtful answers.
  • Feedback Session: After the mock interview, you will receive detailed feedback on your performance. Use this feedback to identify areas of improvement and prepare effectively for your next real interview.
